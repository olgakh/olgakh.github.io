About Us

Our unique online store features the current and latest trends from the interior design world as well as popular iconic products and design classics. Popular products include, chairs from Kartell and other leading brands such as Alessi, LSA International glassware, Flos Lighting and Umbra. It’s incredibly easy and hassle-free to buy Kartell furniture online with Design 55.
